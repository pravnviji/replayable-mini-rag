Product: InsightBoard
InsightBoard is an analytics workspace for operations teams. It supports dashboards, scheduled reports,
and anomaly alerts. Scheduled reports can be exported as CSV or PDF. The platform retains event data
for 13 months on the standard plan and 36 months on the enterprise plan.

Authentication supports email-password, SSO via SAML, and SCIM provisioning on enterprise plans.
The public API exposes read-only access for dashboards and reports.
